Jinyeop Song

sjinyeop@gmail.com / Google Scholar / GitHub / LinkedIn / X

I am a final-year PhD student in the MIT Physics Department, advised by Prof. Jeff Gore. I am a former MATS scholar and MIT-IBM Watson AI Lab research intern. This summer, I will be joining Xaira Therapeutics as a full-time AI Researcher in San Francisco.

I work on the Science of AI and AI for Science. My recent work studies neural scaling laws, in-context learning, and LLM agents, while also building foundation-model and agentic systems for biology.
